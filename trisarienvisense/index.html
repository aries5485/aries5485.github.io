About TrisariEnviSense (TES)

TrisariEnviroSense is a software platform designed to collect, process, and display environmental data in real time. The application can be accessed through a web browser and allows users to view and analyze data from a variety of sensors and devices, such as temperature and humidity sensors, air quality monitors, and weather stations. The data is collected continuously and displayed in a user-friendly interface, making it easy for users to track and understand changes in the environment. The application can be customized to display specific data sets and to alert users when certain conditions or thresholds are met. It is a valuable tool for businesses, organizations, and individuals who need to monitor and understand the environmental conditions of their facilities or locations in real time.
