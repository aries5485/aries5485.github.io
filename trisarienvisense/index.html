About TrisariEnviSense (TES)

TrisariEnviSense is a software platform designed to collect, process, and display environmental data in real time. The application can be accessed through a web browser and allows users to view and analyze data from a variety of sensors and devices, such as temperature and humidity sensors, air quality monitors, and weather stations. The data is collected continuously and displayed in a user-friendly interface, making it easy for users to track and understand changes in the environment. The application can be customized to display specific data sets and to alert users when certain conditions or thresholds are met. It is a valuable tool for businesses, organizations, and individuals who need to monitor and understand the environmental conditions of their facilities or locations in real time.

Awesome Features

Chart Reading

The software allows you to read the data received in form of various charts that can be used to assess the data. See the data on specific time range, set the parameters you need, and combine multiple charts to one aggregrate historic data that can be analyzed further.

Customizable Map Viewer

Customize your map in interactive, dynamic way that suits your needs. Add markers, attachments, styles, layers, and more to fit how your map looks based on what your need.

Dashboard Management

Manage all of your information needs in a coordinated, customizable dashboard. Create dashboard in a simple yet eye-catching view with chart, gauge, images, maps and more that can be resized to your liking.

Notification & Alarm

Receive important notification and alarms regarding your data in realtime manner. Control your information flow depending on the categories of the alarms.

Data Export

Export your data in various format as you need, allowing them to be processed further. Choose between exporting raw data or generated report data in your desired parameter to satisfy your needs.

Data Logger Compatibility

Wide range of compatibility with data loggers around the world to be used with the software, such as DataTaker, OTT, Campbell, and other data logger with common format (.csv, .ntt, and .json). Connect and control the data logger's data acquirement with the settings provided.

Company Customization

This software is for you. Why don't you customize it in your way? Create a personalized interface based on your company. Customize your icons, image, graphics and text over the interface, creating an interface that is unlike any others.

CCTV View

Implement the view of your installed cameras directly to you. Configure and sort the image received to a quick viewing media gallery, and associate the images received to specific station.
